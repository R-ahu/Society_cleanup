About Us

Our Mission

We are committed to fostering sustainable, cleaner, and greener communities by mobilizing citizens for local cleanup drives and promoting long-term environmental awareness.

Who We Are

Community Clean-Up is a civic-led initiative that unites students, professionals, environmentalists, and everyday citizens to collectively tackle pollution and urban waste challenges.

Our Journey

Started as grassroots meetups in local neighborhoods, we have grown into a nationwide environmental movement, inspiring thousands to contribute to a cleaner India.

Our Impact

From reducing plastic waste in urban parks to organizing large-scale cleanups in polluted cities, our measurable achievements have been recognized by local authorities and supported by environmental experts.

Working with Environmentalists

We actively collaborate with environmental scientists, sustainability advocates, and civic planners to ensure that our efforts contribute to lasting ecological improvements and informed community action.

Education & Advocacy

Beyond cleanups, we conduct workshops, school programs, and public awareness campaigns to educate citizens about pollution prevention, recycling, and sustainable living practices.
